Grilled Cheese

Description

This is a simple and very easy recipe for anyone no matter how great or bad they are at cooking.

Ingredients

  • bread
  • your choice of cheese
  • butter

Steps

  1. Grab two slice of bread and butter one side of each
  2. Heat up pan, once pan is hot place first slice of bread on pan, then desired amount of cheese, then other slice of bread
  3. Cook bottom of sandwich till golden brown, then flip sandwich and repeat
  4. Once both sides are cooked, take off pan and enjoy!
