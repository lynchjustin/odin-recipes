Chipotle Chicken

Description

This chipotle chicken recipe is almost an indentical recreation of Chipotle's chicken. This chicken is just as juicy and as flavorful as the chicken you would get at the store, for a fraction of the price. It's very important that you follow the directions carefully and that you buy boneless/skinless chicken thighs.

Ingredients

  • 6 to 8 chicken thights
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 4 minced garlic cloves
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 tbs chile powder
  • 2 tbs olive oil
  • 7 ounces adobo sauce
  • 2 to 4 chipotle peppers
  • 3/4 cup water

Steps

  1. Trim excess fat from chicken thighs
  2. Add wet and dry ingredients into blender and blend
  3. Add marinade and chicken thighs into freezer bag and store in fridge for at least 6 hours
  4. Remove chicken from fridge and let sit out for 30 minutes
  5. Heat up pan on stove and cook chicken on medium heat for 8 minutes each side
  6. Take cooked chicken and let rest for 5 minutes
  7. Dice up chicken and serve!
