Hamburgers

Description

This is a quick and delicious recipe if you want a nice homecooked meal, but are short on time.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup of breadcrumbs
  • a few dashes of basil, oregano, parsely, salt and pepper
  • olive oil

    • Steps

    1. Add all ingrendients into a mixing bowl
    2. Combine all ingredients into one giant ball
    3. Form about 4 to 5 individual balls from giant ball
    4. Form each ball into a patty
    5. Brush each patty with olive oil
    6. Cook each side on pan for 6 to 8 minutes
    7. Serve!
